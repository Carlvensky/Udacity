Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is considere one of the greatest Soccer/Football player of all time.

He won 3 back-toback Premier League with them and helped them won the UEFA Champions league by 2008, he was the most expensive player after moving to Real Madrid in 2009.
